*   **Security & Safety:** The MCP servers **must** be the gatekeepers, validating all actions against business rules.
*   **Human-in-the-Loop:** Mandate confirmation for all changes. Position Claude as an expert assistant, not an autonomous system.
*   **Data Quality:** The AI is only as good as its knowledge base and network data. Invest in cleaning and structuring internal documentation.
*   **Change Management:** Train senior specialists to "teach" the AI and new staff to trust and use it effectively.

# Conclusion

This initiative transforms a major constraint into a strategic advantage. The client is not just replacing retiring experts; they are building a scalable system that institutionalizes knowledge, accelerates onboarding, improves operational efficiency, and increases development velocity. The MCP-based AI Copilot is a pragmatic and powerful solution to a critical industry problem.
