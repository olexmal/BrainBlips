# Project Overview

:::info Executive Summary
The **Optical Network AI Copilot** is a strategic initiative that transforms the challenge of retiring optical network experts into a competitive advantage by leveraging AI to institutionalize knowledge and accelerate team capabilities.
:::

## Introduction

The telecommunications industry is experiencing an exciting transformation with the accelerating retirement of seasoned optical network experts, creating opportunities for innovative solutions. 

The client is enthusiastic about AI capabilities and actively seeking an AI facilitator solution that can enhance their systems and empower their staff with advanced AI-powered tools and knowledge.

This document proposes a strategic initiative to capitalize on this opportunity: the **Optical Network AI Copilot**. This solution leverages a modern AI agentic framework (Anthropic's Model Context Protocol - MCP) to encapsulate institutional knowledge and network APIs, empowering staff with cutting-edge AI capabilities. 

The solution is dual-purpose, designed to enhance both **Network Operations** and **Software Development/Testing** teams with advanced AI tools.
